ABOUT US

India’s Largest and Fastest Growing Indoor Cricket Academy

At Strike The Ball, we are passionate about cricket and dedicated to helping players of all levels sharpen their skills in a professional, safe, and comfortable indoor environment. Whether you're just starting out or pushing toward elite performance, our state-of-the-art indoor nets provide the perfect setting to train year-round—rain or shine.

Founded by cricket lovers for cricket lovers, we believe in creating an inclusive and supportive space where every player can grow. Our facility features high-quality practice nets, professional-grade flooring, and top-tier equipment, making it ideal for individual practice, team sessions, or coaching clinics.

Message from the Founder

Welcome to Strike The Ball – where passion meets precision, and every cricketer finds their edge.

When I started Strike The Ball, the vision was simple: to create a space where players could train like professionals, no matter the weather or season. What began as a dream has now grown into a thriving community with five centers across Gurgaon, each designed to provide the best indoor cricket training experience possible.

Cricket is more than just a sport – it’s discipline, teamwork, and relentless pursuit of improvement. At Strike The Ball, we’ve built an arena where that spirit lives and breathes every day. Whether you're an aspiring youngster, a seasoned club player, or just someone who loves the game, our doors are open for you.

This journey wouldn’t have been possible without the love and support of our incredible cricketing family. Thank you for trusting us to be a part of your training journey. Together, let’s continue to raise the bar and strike the ball with purpose.

See you in the Nets!

Suraj Dhama, Founder
